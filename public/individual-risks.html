Individual Risks

This section presents insurance solutions designed for individuals and professionals to protect their homes, activities, and assets against common and mandatory risks.

Multi-Risk Home Insurance (MRH)

MRH

For individuals living in a residential property, whether owners or tenants. Comprehensive protection for household risks and liabilities.

Covered Risks
Fire, explosions, lightning
Theft and vandalism
Water damage
Natural disasters (Cat-Nat)
Glass breakage
Family civil liability
Mandatory coverage for all homeowners and tenants in Algeria.

Multi-Risk Professional Insurance

MRP

Designed for merchants, craftsmen, liberal professionals, and enterprises to secure their business activities and premises against a wide range of risks.

Covered Risks
Fire, explosions, and related risks
Storm damage and climatised signs
Theft and vandalism
Business interruption
Water damage
Professional civil liability
Glass breakage
Equipment breakdown
Recommended for all businesses, offices and liberal professions.

Natural Disaster Insurance (Cat-Nat)

Cat-Nat

Mandatory for all owners of real estate property (residential, commercial, or industrial) located in Algeria. Guarantees compensation in the event of a natural catastrophe declared by the government.

Covered Events
Fire Propagation
Storms and violent winds
Floods and inundations
Landslides
Earthquakes
Drought-related ground movement
Tsunamis
Other declared natural events
Mandatory coverage against major natural events for all property owners.

Transport Insurance

This section covers insurance solutions related to the transport of goods and vehicles, by land, sea, or air.

Pleasure Craft Insurance

Corps de Bateau

For owners of pleasure boats seeking protection against maritime risks. Covers the vessel itself and third-party liability during navigation.

Covered Risks
Damage to the boat within territory
Third-party liability
Theft and vandalism
Fire and explosion
Personal accident for passengers
Towing and assistance at sea
Recommended for all owners of private boats and leisure vessels.

Air Cargo Insurance

Fret Aérien

For businesses shipping fragile, sensitive or high-value goods by air. Covers the merchandise from the moment of loading to final delivery.

Covered Risks
Accidental damage during flight
Theft or non-delivery
Damage during loading/unloading
Aircraft accident or emergency
Water damage from firefighting
Customs seizure or loss
Recommended for importers, exporters and freight forwarders.

Goods Insurance (Public Transport)

TMD Public

For companies using public transport services to move their goods. Covers merchandise entrusted to public carriers throughout the journey.

Covered Risks
Damage during transit
Theft while in custody of public carrier
Fire or accident involving the vehicle
Overturning or derailment
Loading and unloading damage
Delay-related losses
Recommended for all merchants and companies shipping goods via carriers.

Goods Insurance (Private Transport)

TMD Privé

For businesses transporting their own goods with their own vehicles. Protects the merchandise against all risks during private road transport.

Covered Risks
Road accidents involving the vehicle
Theft following violent entry
Theft following vehicle entry
Fire and explosion
Overturning and collision
Loading and unloading damage
Recommended for businesses with their own transport fleet.

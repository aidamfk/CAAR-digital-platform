About CAAR

CAAR is a national insurance company covering individuals, professionals, and institutions.

Our Mandate

Mission

Develop accessible insurance solutions distributed across CAAR's 298 agencies nationwide, combining state resources and market expertise.

Participation

Developing inclusiveness and opportunities for the widest possible national financial levels. Showing leadership at national levels in projects.

Resource Investments

Continuously invest in assets to better organise, promote and implement coverage to all citizens, and participate in promoting good communities.

Our Activities
  • Individual insurance solutions including home, auto and personal liability
  • Professional and commercial coverage for businesses of all sizes

  • Industrial risk management for factories, plants and infrastructure

  • Reinsurance services supporting national capacity
  • Online subscription and secure CIB digital payment services

About CAAR

CAAR is a national insurance and reinsurance company serving individuals, professionals, and institutions.

Dear partners,
Dear colleagues,

Assuming the presidency of the Algerian Insurance and Reinsurance Company is for me an immense honor and a responsibility that I embrace with humility and determination.

It is also a return to an institution to which I remain deeply attached, and whose history merges with that of the economic and social development of our country.

For more than six decades, the CAAR has embodied solidity, trust and commitment. It has accompanied the major changes in the national economy, protected generations of citizens, and supported the ambitions of thousands of companies. This heritage is our pride, but also our responsibility: that of keeping it alive, strengthening it, and transmitting it.

Today, a new stage opens.

Our ambition is clear: to bring the CAAR into an era of modernity and agility, while remaining faithful to its identity as a reference company, close to its clients and faithful to its values.

This involves:

• Digitize our services to offer our policyholders a smooth, fast and accessible experience;
• Strengthen our proximity with our customers, by placing their expectations and satisfaction at the heart of our priorities;
• Valuing our internal talents, by encouraging innovation, continuous training and operational excellence;
• Reaffirm our leadership role, adopting international best practices and anticipating market developments.

This transformation will not be a break, but an ambitious continuity.

It will build on the solid foundations of CAAR, while instilling a new dynamic focused on sustainable performance, social I measure the human richness, expertise and professionalism that exist within our teams. It is together, in a spirit of cohesion, responsibility and openness, that we will write the next chapter of the CAAR and restore all the lustre it deserves.

To our employees, partners and clients:

Thank you for your trust.

The future of CAAR is built with you, and it is with you that we will face the challenges ahead, responsibility and value creation for all our stakeholders.

Auto Insurance

This section presents insurance solutions for vehicles, including mandatory coverage and optional guarantees to protect drivers, passengers, and third parties against damages.

Motor Insurance

RC Auto

Mandatory for all vehicles registered in Algeria. Covers civil liability towards third parties in the event of a traffic accident, including bodily injury and property damage caused to others.

Covered Risks
Bodily injury to third parties
Material damage to third parties
Passenger liability coverage
Hit-and-run accident coverage
Legal defense and recourse
Uninsured driver protection
Mandatory for all vehicles circulating on public roads in Algeria.

Roadside Assistance

Assistance

Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, throughout the national territory. An essential complement to your auto insurance that ensures you are never stranded in case of breakdown or accident.

Services Included
Vehicle towing to nearest garage
On-site mechanical assistance
Replacement vehicle provision
Hotel accommodation if stranded
Repatriation of vehicle and passengers
24/7 telephone support
Optional guarantee — highly recommended for all vehicle owners.

Glass Breakage Assistance

Bris de Glace

For vehicle owners seeking to cover glass-related damages. Covers repair or replacement of all glass elements of your vehicle.

Covered Elements
Windshield damage
Side and rear windows
Sunroof glass panel
Rear-view mirror glass
Headlight and indicator lenses
Emergency replacement service
Optional guarantee — available as an add-on to any auto policy.

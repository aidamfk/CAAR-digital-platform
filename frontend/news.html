What to do after a road accident?

Knowing the correct steps to take after a road accident helps protect your safety and ensures a smoother insurance process.

  • Secure the accident area and ensure everyone's safety
  • Contact the authorities if necessary
  • Document damages and collect information from all parties
  • Inform your insurance company as soon as possible

How to properly protect your home?

Home insurance helps protect your property and belongings against common risks such as fire, water damage, and theft.

  • Identify the main risks affecting your home
  • Insure both the building and its contents when applicable
  • Accurately declare the value of your belongings
  • Regularly update your contract after renovations or new purchases
Learn more→

Why is business insurance essential?

Business insurance protects companies against operational, financial, and liability risks that may threaten their activity.

  • Identify risks related to your business activity and sector
  • Insure company assets: buildings, equipment, and stock
  • Protect your business against third-party liability
  • Review coverage regularly as the business grows
Learn more→

Understanding the basics of insurance

Insurance is a financial protection mechanism that helps individuals and businesses manage risks and unforeseen events.

  • Understand the difference between mandatory and optional coverage
  • Know what risks are covered and what exclusions apply
  • Choose coverage adapted to your personal or professional situation
  • Respect contract conditions to avoid claim rejection
